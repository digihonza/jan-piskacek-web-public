About me

I am a software developer currently based in Prague, Czech Republic. I have a 10+ year experience developing web applications that help companies solve their business problems.

I am passionate about new technologies, clean code, delivering impactful solutions, and asking questions that others didn't think to ask but wanted to know. I enjoy working on projects that challenge me to grow as a developer and deliver value to my clients.

In my free time I enjoy photography, traveling, playing music, and a good cup of coffee or a mug of good beer. All of those activities are made better when shared with my wife. :-)

My Development Philosophy

Custom software development is expensive, so if it's worth building, it's worth building to last. In order for software to last, it must meet current requirements and be easily adaptable as requirements change which they are bound to do. I help develop lasting software by ...

  • asking a lot of questions throughout the entire project
    • First to make sure that we are building the right thing, then to make sure we are building it right way.
  • developing with change and future maintainability in mind
    • Focus on code readability, flexible architecture, and automated testing.
